    Epidemic prevention measures：

    For those passengers come from oversea countries, the HES code should be received and presented whenever required in the territory of Turkey;
    Details as followed: https://register.health.gov.tr/

    To the attention of passengers with flights from Denmark and South Africa to Turkey: Only the citizens of the Republic of Turkey, and the residence permit and blue card holders with Turkey are admitted to the flights from Denmark and South Africa to Turkey. All passengers (6 and above) must present a negative PCR test result with samples given 72 hours prior to their flights. Passengers departing from these countries must go to their declared place of residence upon arrival and self-isolate for 7 days. If the result of the PCR test to be conducted following the 7-day isolation is negative, the self-isolation will end. If the test result is positive, contact tracing teams will respond and monitor your status.
